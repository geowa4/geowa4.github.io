Bio

By trade, I'm a Software Engineer at Brand Networks. I've worked on projects both big and small—some spending millions of dollars buying ads on social channels like Facebook and Twitter. My goal is to be the mystical "10x engineer" by developing tools and processes that turn 10 1x engineers into 2x engineers.

Most of my spare time is filled with learning new technologies to assist me in my work. Recent efforts have centered around highly scalable and available distributed systems using Ansible, Docker, and etcd to name a few. Beyond that, I've begun researching Apache Spark to process increasingly large data sets originating from medical, IoT, and the data originating from modern distributed systems.

Extre Curriculars

While learning is a passion, I also like to get out into the physical world. On weekends, I like to play disc golf at the local courses. Rochester is lucky enough to have over a dozen worth playing. If you're interested, you can find out more a GRDGC.

Lastly, while building software and infrastructure is fun, sometimes I need to build something I can hold. To satisfy this desire, I've set up a small woodshop in my basement. One nice side effect of this is that I can build what I want for less than I'd probably pay for it. If only software always worked that way.
